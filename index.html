wiki2man

Created a simple utility to convert mediawiki entries into man pages. Developed primarily for adding wiki pages to the standard set of man pages on Computer Science House systems.

DC Area Crime Map 2011

Paired up crime data sets from the DC, Maryland, and Virgina State police with county maps of the area in ArcGIS, and produced a robust data visualization. I had a good time working on this project, and it was a lot of fun to see patterns emerge that were entirely unanticipated (Pornography charges in western Virgina are significantly higher per capita), along with a good deal of local knowlege confirmed (Richmond is a hotbed for crime.)
